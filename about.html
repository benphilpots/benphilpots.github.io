About Me

Hi, I'm Ben! I’m a senior at the University of Massachusetts Amherst studying Economics and Environmental Science in the Commonwealth Honors College. My academic work focuses on renewable energy policy, environmental data analysis, and applied economics.

I’ve worked on projects involving GIS mapping, linear regressions, time-series data analysis in R, environmental research, and more. If you're curious, you can take a deeper dive into what I've done in the Projects section (top right of the browser). Once I graduate, I plan to continue developing my career in the intersection of energy, environmental policy, and data-driven decision-making.

Outside of coursework, I enjoy learning technical skills (such as designing a website with HTML). I also like eating, sleeping, and rock climbing.
